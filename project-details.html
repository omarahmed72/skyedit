El Centro Mall | New Capital

District: New Administrative Capital Request a Zoom Meeting

El Centro Mall New Capital is a landmark mixed-use development by Empire State Developments, strategically located in the heart of Downtown New Administrative Capital on plot MU5-31, with direct views over the Central Park and close proximity to the Government, Financial, and Business districts. The project features a modern architectural design with expansive open views and dedicated large portion of its land to green spaces, creating a balanced and attractive environment. Comprising a ground floor and fourteen upper floors, the mall includes a well-organized mix of commercial retail spaces, medical clinics, administrative offices, and hotel units.
